Toyota Car Selection

Shortlist a Toyota Car when you have conflicting goals

Overview

This App will help people make decisions on Cars when balancing multiple objectives using Bluemix Watson TradeOff Analytics.

Point of View

It uses Bluemix TradeOff Analytics service that uses a mathematical filtering technique called “Pareto Optimization,” that enables users to explore tradeoffs when considering multiple criteria for a single decision. When your company makes decisions, how many factors need to be considered? What’s the process like? How do you know when you’ve found the best option? With Tradeoff Analytics, users can avoid lists of endless options and identify the right option by considering multiple objectives
