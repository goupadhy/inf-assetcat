Location Analytics with IBM Cognos

Location Analytics is one of the industry solutions with IBM cognos to get insight from geospatial data.

Overview

Location intelligence (LI) is the process of deriving meaningful insight from geospatial data relationships to solve a particular problem. It involves layering multiple data sets spatially and/or chronologically, for easy reference on a map.

Point of View

By adding geographic location to business data and mapping it, organizations can dramatically enhance their insights into tabular data. Maps and spatial analytics provide a whole new context that is simply not possible with tables and charts. This context can almost immediately help users discover new understandings and more effectively communicate and collaborate using maps as a common language.
